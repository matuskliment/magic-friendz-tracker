About

Magic Friendz

Magic Friendz is a free web app for Magic: The Gathering Commander playgroups. It helps your table log pod results, track commander and player Elo, revisit match history, and settle friendly debates with real numbers, scoped to your group, not a global ranking site.

Your group. Your meta. Your numbers.

Unlike apps that rank decks or players across everyone on the platform, Magic Friendz gives each playgroup its own match history, leaderboards, and Elo. Ratings come from your pod's games only, so they reflect your table's power level, house rules, and local meta, not strangers you will never sit down with.

Built by Mat Kliment

Magic Friendz was created by Mat Kliment for kitchen-table Commander nights: fast match logging, shareable group links, and insights that grow with your league. The app runs at edhstatstracker.xyz.

What you can do

  • Log Commander matches: winners, losers, pilots, decks, elimination details
  • View commander and player leaderboards with playgroup Elo (your pod only)
  • Browse match history, session recaps, and playgroup insights
  • Share a group link so anyone can view stats; sign in to log games
  • Export your group data as JSON or CSV

Getting started

Open your group with a URL like https://edhstatstracker.xyz/?group=myplaygroup. Create a new group from the homepage if you are starting fresh. Editors sign in with a magic link, no password required.
